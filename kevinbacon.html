Kevin Bacon: Six Degrees of Separation

Back

Goal

To connect an actor to Kevin Bacon through a trail of actors who appeared in the same movie in less than six steps.
The number of steps is referred to as the Bacon Number.

Approach

  1. Develop a graph that represents the data for this game. This will be held inside of a dictionary object.
    • An actor at each vertex
    • An edge between them if they have appeared in the same movie together.
  2. Using Breadth First Search find the shortest path between Kevin Bacon and the given actor.
  3. Used Depth First Search to compare results and run-time.

Tools Used
